About Us

DCB was started in 2022, the primary aim is to give youths industry ready skills unique from the usual local skills, we aim to master skills in multi-cloud environments such as AWS Cloud, Microsoft Azure and Google GCP. Currently we working with youths from disadvantage background but, are bright did not proceed to the next education level for one reason or the other.

Once they graduate, they will be working as Cloud Engineers, Solutions Architects, DevOps engineers and Systems Administrators. Locally the demand in cloud skills rapidly growing and the need has doubled, given the COVID 19 scenario that took industry by a shock, and this make work from home a need and want.

Organization moved their operations to cloud and therefore the need for resources to operate the infrastructure In the recent past machine learning and artificial intelligence is greatly gaining attraction and having the right data in import, our team have gone through session to annotate, model training using various tool and application and we are working with organization to train their AI and machine learning tools to power their AI and Machine learning devices, we partnered with Next Step foundation who funded the program
